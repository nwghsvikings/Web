About Northwest Robotics

Founded in 2015, Northwest Robotics (Official name: Robo Vikings #9990) is a team of passionate high school students interested in the aspects of robotics, programming, engineering, financing, and more.

Our mission is to create robots to compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge and also serve our community in Guilford County through STEM showcases and workshops.
